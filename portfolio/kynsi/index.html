Kynsi Logo, Corporate Identity and Website

Objective

We faced the challenge of developing the name, logo, corporate identity and website that would satisfy the highly exigent target audience of the nail salon.

We organized a contest to choose the name for the salon. Our partners from Brandberry.net helped us to develop the company’s logo and corporate identity.

Then came the website to support the brand’s concise and minimalistic style.

The website was also optimized for correct and smooth visualization on Apple iPad and iPhone.
