Shopping center "Akvarel" website development

Goals

To develop a shopping center site in 2 versions: one for the sale, second for the rental of mall spaces

Panoramic 3d-render demonstrates advertising opportunities of the mall's facade and the unique animation of the home page highlights the benefits of SC

Parameters of the selected room are shown in a convenient form

A set of pages was developed, giving a comprehensive view of the proposed areas, projected income and payback

Due to the difference of the target audiences, rental site is more colorful and has different font solutions
